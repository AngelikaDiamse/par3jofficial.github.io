ABOUT PAR3J

Par3J Golf Shop and Repair is a sport shop, established in 2018 offering golf goods alike; shoes, apparel, clubs, and services like repairing damaged golf clubs.

It is inspired by the owner's past job experiences in different successful golf companies mainly, Mizuno and Ping. The shop is run by the partners; Romeo and Jayson. The main goal of the company is to sell quality golf goods to customers and aid the growing number of golfers that seeks the right and best golf services in the city of Angeles, Pampanga.
